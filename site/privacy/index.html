Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2025 · Effective date: April 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MSPNova ("we", "us", "Nova") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit mspnova.com or use the MSPNova platform.

1. What we collect

Marketing site visitors. Anonymized analytics: page views, referrer, viewport size, broad geo (country/region). We use a privacy-respecting analytics product that does not set tracking cookies or fingerprint visitors.

Demo and contact forms. Information you submit: name, work email, company, role, PSA, team size, and your free-text response. Used only to schedule the demo and follow up.

Customer accounts. Authentication metadata via Microsoft Entra ID (object ID, email, group memberships). We do not collect or store passwords directly.

Customer ticket data. Read from your PSA via API, processed in-memory for classification, written back to your PSA. We persist only ticket metadata (IDs, classifications, history) — not ticket bodies.

2. What we do with it

  • Operate and improve the MSPNova platform.
  • Respond to inquiries and schedule demos.
  • Send transactional product emails (e.g., uptime alerts, billing notices).
  • Send occasional product update emails — opt-out at any time, link in every email.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. What we don't do

  • Sell your data — never.
  • Use your ticket content to train AI models (zero-retention with our AI providers).
  • Share data with advertisers, brokers, or third parties beyond named sub-processors.
  • Use cross-site tracking cookies.

4. Sub-processors

We use a small set of named sub-processors to operate the platform. The current list is published at /security/subprocessors and is updated 30 days before any material change.

5. Your rights

If you're in the EU, EEA, UK, California, or any jurisdiction with applicable data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, port, or delete your personal data. Email privacy@mspnova.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

6. Retention

Marketing form data: 24 months from last contact. Customer account data: for the life of the contract plus 90 days. Ticket metadata: same. Audit logs: 24 months.

7. Cookies

We set one strictly necessary session cookie when you log in to the customer portal. We do not set marketing or third-party tracking cookies on the marketing site.

8. Security

All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Security disclosures: security@mspnova.com.

9. Children

The MSPNova service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify customers by email and post a notice on this page. Non-material changes (typo fixes, restructuring) may happen without notice.
