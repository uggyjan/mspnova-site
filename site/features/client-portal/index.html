Inside the portal

Six surfaces. All optional. All client-configurable.

Ticket history

Open + closed tickets, filtered to the user or their org. Status, owner, last update, public comments only.

New ticket form

Configurable per-client form. Required fields, hidden fields, attachments, urgency capture. Auto-routed by Nova on submit.

SLA dashboard

Last-30-days response time, resolution time, SLA hit rate. Hide or show per contract type.

CSAT widget

Per-ticket satisfaction survey. Aggregate score visible to client owners. Trends over time.

Asset register

User-facing view of devices, M365 licenses, software entitlements. Pulls from CIPP + Autotask.

Knowledge base

Per-client KB articles. AI-suggested matches when users open new tickets. Deflect simple issues.

/ White-label

Your logo. Your colors. Your domain.

The portal lives at portal.yourmsp.com (or any subdomain you choose). Your wordmark in the header, your brand colors throughout, your support contact info in the footer. Clients never see "MSPNova" — they see you.

  • Custom domain with managed SSL
  • Logo upload, primary + accent color tokens
  • Header / footer text fully editable
  • Optional per-client co-branding (your logo + their logo)
/ Authentication

SSO that actually works on day one.

Most clients use Microsoft 365. The portal supports M365 SSO out of the box — clients click "Sign in with Microsoft," get redirected, return authenticated. SAML and Google Workspace also supported. Local accounts available for orgs without SSO.

  • Microsoft 365 SSO (Entra ID)
  • Google Workspace SSO
  • SAML 2.0 for everything else
  • RBAC: regular user, manager, billing, admin
