/ Mobile check-in

Tap on arrival. Tap on departure. The PSA gets it.

Techs open Nova on their phone, see today's on-site visits, and tap "Check in" when they arrive. The status updates in dispatch, the time entry starts in Autotask, and the client gets an optional "your tech is on-site" SMS. Check out with a one-line work summary that becomes the time entry note.

  • Geofenced check-in confirms arrival at client site
  • Time entry auto-created in Autotask on check-out
  • Optional client SMS: "Your tech (Dee) is on-site at 9:42 AM"
  • Mileage capture for billable travel
/ Status board

A wall display the dispatcher can actually use.

Live grid of every tech, their current state (on-site, in-office, lunch, PTO), the ticket they're on, and the time they've been on it. Color-coded for overruns. Dispatch sees at a glance who's free to take the next escalation.

  • Color-coded states: green / amber / red for overruns
  • Click any cell to see full ticket + assign work
  • Designed for a 4K dispatch wall — readable from across the room
  • Filter by location, skill, or contract
/ Flash Ticket

A touch panel at the client site. Tap to open a ticket.

For client sites with shared spaces (front desks, conference rooms, kiosks): a 10" touch panel mounted on the wall. Common issue tiles ("Wi-Fi down," "printer jammed," "AV not working") create a triaged Autotask ticket with location auto-filled. No looking up help desk numbers. No Outlook.

  • Configurable tile set per client site
  • Auto-fills location, sublocation, and reporter
  • Optional voice memo for context
  • Confirmation screen with ETA and ticket number
