AI triage

From ticket creation to dispatched, in 30 seconds.

Nova watches Autotask with adaptive polling — 30 seconds during business hours, 5 minutes off-hours, 15 minutes overnight. New tickets get classified across priority, issue type, queue, category, sub-category, work type, and source before the dispatcher sees them. Each suggestion comes with reasoning attached.

  • Adaptive polling — fast when it matters, quiet when it doesn't
  • Reasoning shown for every classification — nothing is a black box
  • One-click override; feedback flows into the next pass
  • BYO AI key — typical run cost is under $1/day

02 /
Needs Attention

One queue. Ranked by urgency, not arrival time.

Composite scoring weighs SLA risk, age, client tier, skill match, and technician scarcity. The Pull Ticket flow presents each tech their next best ticket — anti-cherry-picking is structural, not policy. Each card explains why it needs attention right now.

  • Live SLA countdowns and breach alerts
  • One-click: assign, reply, ask the tech via Teams DM
  • 12-minute triage SLA target, tracked daily
  • Holding lanes for waiting-on-client tickets

Pulse + Field Ops

Daily huddles, briefings, and a mobile status board.

Pulse generates the morning briefing — capacity, on-site schedule, escalations, talking points — and posts it to Teams before standup. Techs check in and out from their phone. The dispatcher sees who's where, who's on lunch, and who's overrun on a job.
