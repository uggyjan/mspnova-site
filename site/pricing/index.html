What it actually costs to run

You bring your own API key. Tokens cost about a coffee a day.

For a typical small MSP running Nova on real ticket volume, expect well under $1/day in AI usage billed directly by your provider. That's roughly $20–$30 per month — and it scales with how much work Nova does, not how many seats you have.
